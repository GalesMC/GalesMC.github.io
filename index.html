Survival Tips — First Things to Do

  • Collect wood immediately (punch trees) for crafting table, sticks, basic tools.
  • Mine stone quickly (stone tools are far more durable than wooden ones).
  • Craft a workbench, build a simple shelter, and light it up to avoid hostile mobs at night.
  • Prioritize a wooden pickaxe → stone pickaxe → furnace so you can cook food and smelt materials.
