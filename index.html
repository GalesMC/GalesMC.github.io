Survival Tips — First Things to Do

  • Collect wood immediately (punch trees) for basic tools.
  • Mine stone quickly to upgrade to stone tools and make a furnace.
  • Craft a workbench, build a small shelter, and light it to avoid hostile mobs at night.
  • Prioritize a wooden pickaxe → stone pickaxe → furnace so you can cook food and smelt resources.
